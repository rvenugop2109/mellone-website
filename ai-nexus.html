Who It's For

Built for leaders who need AI clarity, fast.

AI Nexus is designed specifically for senior decision-makers. You don't need to become a technologist. You need to understand the landscape, identify where AI creates value in your organisation, and drive adoption. That's what AI Nexus delivers.

🏢
Founders & CEOs

You're being asked about AI by your board, your team, and your clients. AI Nexus gives you the clarity to lead confidently and make better strategic decisions.

💼
C-Suite Executives

CFOs, CMOs, COOs, CHROs, functional leaders who need to understand AI's impact on their domain and lead their team's transformation.

🎯
Senior Functional Heads

Directors and VPs who are responsible for transformation in their function and need to move their team forward without becoming AI engineers.

AI Nexus is not for everyone, and that's intentional.

✓ What you do need

No AI knowledge required, we start from first principles. You need a genuine desire to understand AI and drive adoption in your organisation.

Curriculum

5 modules. 20 hours. Real outcomes.

A structured journey from AI fundamentals through to a working implementation roadmap, delivered in live sessions with access to 200+ hours of recorded content.

Module 01 🌐 The AI Landscape: Opportunities & Competitive Advantage
Introduction to AI tools and capabilities Understanding LLMs without the jargon Where AI creates real competitive advantage Hands-on: first prompts, live tools
Module 02 🗺️ AI Strategy Development & Business Case
Identifying high-impact AI opportunities Prioritisation frameworks Building a board-ready business case Peer workshop: your organisation
Module 03 ⚙️ Automating Business Workflows & AI Assistants
Automating common leadership workflows Building your personal AI assistant Tools: Claude, Gemini, Make, Zapier, n8n Hands-on: live automation build
Module 04 🏗️ AI Strategy Implementation
Building on identified use cases Implementation planning and resourcing Change management for AI adoption Hands-on: live business case build
Module 05 🎯 AI Adoption Roadmap & Ethics
Governance structures for AI Ethical AI and responsible deployment Building your 90-day roadmap Presentations and peer review
What You'll Leave With

Not just knowledge. A plan you can act on.

Every AI Nexus graduate leaves with six concrete deliverables, built during the programme, owned by you.

1
AI opportunity map for your organisation
A prioritised view of where AI can create the most value across your functions, built collaboratively during module 2.
2
A compelling AI business case
Structured, board-ready justification for your highest-priority AI initiative, including ROI, timeline, and resource requirements.
3
Working AI workflows and automations
Live automations built during the course, real tools, real outputs you can take back and start using immediately.
4
A 90-day AI implementation roadmap
A practical, sequenced plan for your first 90 days of AI deployment, with milestones, ownership, and success metrics.
5
Personal AI toolkit and prompt library
A curated set of AI tools and prompts built specifically for your leadership workflows, ready to deploy in your organisation.
