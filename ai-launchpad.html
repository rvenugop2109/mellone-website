Who It's For

Built for those at the start of something big.

AI Launchpad is designed for people who are early in their career and want to build real, lasting AI capability, not just surface-level tool familiarity.

🎓
Recent Graduates

Entering the workforce and want to stand out. AI Launchpad gives you a concrete, demonstrable skillset that most graduates simply don't have.

💼
Early-Career Professionals (1–3 years)

You've started your career but want to accelerate it, adding deep AI capability to your existing professional foundation to move faster and do more.

🔄
Career Starters & Switchers

Coming from a non-professional background or pivoting to a new field, AI Launchpad gives you a credible, structured foundation to enter the workforce AI-ready.

⚠️ AI Launchpad may not be right if you...
Have 5+ years of professional experience in your field
Are a founder or C-Suite leader (see AI Nexus)
Are looking for a quick overview rather than deep learning
Are not ready for a sustained 100-hour learning commitment
✓ What you do need

Absolutely no AI knowledge required, we start from zero. All you need is curiosity, commitment, and a career direction you want to build towards. We'll do the rest.

What You'll Leave With

Skills you can show, not just say.

Every outcome from AI Launchpad is something you built during the course, not a list of topics you were taught. Your capstone project is the centrepiece of a portfolio you can take to any employer.

1
A working understanding of how AI actually works
Not just what AI can do, but how LLMs are trained, what tokens and context windows mean, and why AI sometimes gets things wrong.
2
A professional prompt library for your career stream
A curated set of reusable, high-quality prompts built specifically for your field, ready to deploy from your first week on the job.
3
A no-code app or automation you built yourself
A functional application or automated workflow created during the course using AI-powered no-code tools, with no prior development experience needed.
4
AI-generated data analysis and visualisations
A completed data project, from raw dataset through to analysis, charts, and written recommendations, built using AI tools relevant to your stream.
5
Assessed capstone project + certificate
A group project presented to a panel of judges, your most substantial portfolio piece and the basis for your Mellone certificate of completion.
