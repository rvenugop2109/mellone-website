Who It's For

Built for professionals ready to go all in on AI.

AI Elevate is for people who've moved past curiosity and want to become genuinely capable AI practitioners, in their own field, on their own terms.

💼
Mid-Career Professionals (3–15 years experience)

Finance managers, marketers, HR leads, operations professionals, anyone who wants AI to make them significantly more effective in their specific role.

🚀
Startup Founders & Operators

Founders who need to build lean, AI-powered operations and want the hands-on capability to implement, not just commission, AI solutions in their business.

🔄
Career Changers & Upskillers

Professionals pivoting into AI-adjacent roles or those looking to future-proof their current career with deep, practical AI capability.

AI Elevate goes deep. Make sure you're ready for that.

✓ What you do need

No prior AI knowledge required, we start from first principles. You need professional experience in your field and the commitment to see the course through. Everything else we'll teach you.

What You'll Leave With

Deep capability. Real deliverables.

AI Elevate is built around application, not just understanding. Every outcome is something you built, created, or demonstrated, not just something you sat through.

1
A professional-grade prompt library
A curated, reusable library of prompts built specifically for your role and career stream, ready to deploy from day one.
2
Live AI-powered automations
Working automated workflows built during the course, connected to real tools and relevant to your actual job.
3
AI-powered data analysis and visualisation
A completed data project using AI tools, from raw data through to insights, visualisations, and recommendations.
4
A no-code application or tool
A functional web app or internal tool built using no-code AI platforms, no prior development experience needed.
5
Assessed capstone project + certificate
A group capstone project presented to a panel of judges, demonstrating end-to-end AI capability in a real-world context.
