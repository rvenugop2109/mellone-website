Our Services

Three ways we work with organisations.

Every Mellone consulting engagement is scoped to what your organisation actually needs, not a fixed package. These are the three core areas we work across.

🗺️
AI Opportunity Mapping

A structured discovery process that identifies where AI can create the most value in your organisation, prioritised by impact, effort, and readiness. The output is a clear, actionable roadmap, not a generic framework.

  • Process audit across functions and workflows
  • Prioritised opportunity matrix with ROI estimates
  • Quick-win vs long-term implementation roadmap
  • Stakeholder and change readiness assessment
⚙️
Workflow Automation

We identify your most time-consuming, repetitive processes and automate them, connecting tools, building custom automations, and integrating them into your existing workflows.

  • Document processing and report generation
  • Lead management and CRM automation
  • Financial reconciliation and forecasting workflows
  • HR onboarding, screening, and communication
🤖
End-to-End Implementation

For organisations ready to move beyond pilots, we design, build, integrate, and manage full AI solutions that deliver measurable results and remain in use.

  • Custom AI agents and internal chatbots
  • Cross-function AI workflow integration
  • Governance, compliance, and ethical AI frameworks
  • Training and change management for adoption
